Boishakhi Utshob

Saturday, 4:30 - 7:30 PM
By BUCuC
BRAC University Garden Area

Boishakh — a season of light, color, and renewal. BRAC University welcomed Bengali New Year 1432 with a campus transformed into a vibrant canvas of celebration.

The day began with a joyful Boishakhi procession bright smiles, colorful portraits, and hearts full of festive spirit. Under the banyan tree, music and rhythm set the perfect tone, followed by traditional village games and a lively flash mob that spread the energy of Boishakh across campus.

Evening brought an enchanting cultural program — folk songs, graceful dances, and heartfelt recitations reflecting the soul of Bengal. The Boishakhi fair offered traditional foods, handicrafts, and the colors of Bengal in every detail, from sarees and panjabis to intricate alpana designs.

More than just a day, this Boishakh was a heartfelt celebration of culture, heritage, and beauty. The BRAC University Cultural Club warmly thanks everyone who made it truly memorable.

Event Highlights

  • Vibrant Boishakhi procession with colorful portraits and smiles
  • Traditional music and rhythms under the banyan tree
  • Village games reconnecting with Bengal’s roots
  • Energetic flash mob spreading festive cheer
  • Enchanting evening of folk songs, dances, and recitations
  • Boishakhi fair with traditional food, crafts, and alpana art
